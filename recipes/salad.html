CUCUMBER AND ONION SALAD

It's a cool, fresh side dish for any meal.

Ingredients

  • 7 cups sliced cucumbers
  • salt as needed
  • 1 cup sliced onions
  • 1 cup sliced green bell pepper
  • 2 cups white sugar
  • 1 cup white vinegar
  • 1 tablespoon celery seed

Directions

  1. Place cucumber slices in a colander and liberally sprinkle salt over cucumbers; set aside until water starts to release from cucumbers, about 15 minutes
  2. Drain and rinse cucumbers.
  3. Mix cucumbers, onions, and green bell pepper together in a bowl.
  4. Whisk sugar, vinegar, celery seed, and 2 teaspoons salt together in a bowl until dressing is smooth
  5. pour over cucumber mixture and stir until evenly coated.
